Rules
1. This ticket allows only 1 person and is valid for single entry.

8. For security reasons, food items and water bottles are not allowed inside the venue.
9. Drinking water & food are available inside the show premises.
10. Once you leave the pronite arena, re-entry will not be permitted. Please plan accordingly.
11. Entry of children below 12 years of age is not allowed.
