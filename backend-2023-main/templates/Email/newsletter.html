IIT Delhi presents RendezvousX: 'A Contemporary Fantasia'
Where cultures blend, and the modern meets the traditional - coming to you this October!
Read in Substack
Dear Reader,
It's that time of year again when the enchantment of Rendezvous: IITD's annual cultural fest sweeps through Delhi-NCR! This year, Rendezvous is all set to happen from the 5th to the 8th of October 2023!
Over 160,000 attendees from more than 1,000 colleges flocked to Rendezvous last time, making it a resounding success. This festival is renowned for its cultural and musical performances and Pronites where acclaimed national artists such as Nucleya, The Local Train and Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy have graced the stage. Our illustrious history also includes hosting distinguished speakers like Aman Maheshwari and Ashneer Grover, adding intellectual vibrance to its celebration of creativity and talent.
RDV'23 is wrapped up, and RDV'24 is on the horizon for the next year. In the middle of this, we wanted to embrace a new, evolved identity while returning to our old normal - honouring the cherished Rendezvous legacy. Hence, we're back bigger and brighter as RendezvousX!

'Contemporary Fantasia' - This Year's Theme
In keeping with our tradition of pushing boundaries, this year's theme is 'Contemporary Fantasia'.
Fantasia: A thing composed of a mixture of different forms or styles. A captivating blend of art, music and imagination - a cultural symphony that transcends boundaries.
With this Contemporary Fantasia, we're bringing you an unforgettable fusion of modern creativity and rich cultural heritage. Expect immersive art, electrifying musical performances, and a celebration of diverse cultures like never before.
But Rendezvous isn't just about performances and entertainment; it's also a platform to showcase your talents and compete with the best.

Unleash your inner competitive spirit!
At RendezvousX, our lineup of competitions is as diverse as the talents they showcase. From electrifying dance-offs and soul-stirring music battles to intellectually stimulating debates, contemporary art exhibitions, and innovative tech showdowns, our competitions span a spectrum of disciplines to provide a platform for every passion. Whether you're an artist, an engineer, a debater, or a dancer, there's a competition that will challenge and celebrate your unique skills and creativity.
As the countdown to RDVX begins, we invite you to stay tuned for more updates and announcements. We're excited to provide an exuberant experience for all attendees and participants this October.
Join us to experience the RDV magic and create lifelong memories to be treasured in your hearts.
Stay tuned for the magic!
